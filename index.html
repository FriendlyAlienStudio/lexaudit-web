Methodology

Every LexAudit report follows a structured analytical process designed to make contractual reasoning explicit, repeatable, and reviewable.

01 — Structured Fact Extraction

Key contract facts are extracted before legal analysis begins.

02 — Contracts as Future Relationships

Contracts are models of evolving commercial relationships, not merely collections of clauses.

03 — ARL Framework

Asset → Relationship → Lifecycle → Future Scenarios.

04 — Professional Memorandum

Findings are presented as legal work product, not software output.

Agreements are organized around assets, relationships, and future scenarios. The result is transparent, repeatable, and suitable for professional legal review.
